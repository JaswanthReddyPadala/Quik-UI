Badge

Image Badges

The badge component has 4 sizes, badge-xl (5rem), badge-lg (4rem), badge-md (3rem), badge-sm (2rem), and badge-sm (1rem).

This badge component also have 3 different status, online, offline, dnd.

Icon Badges

These are the badges with icons and span element at right top. These are mostly used as notification icons, message icons, etc.
